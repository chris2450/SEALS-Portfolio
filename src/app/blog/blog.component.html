Today we read the Art of War in class and I pulled lots of strategies and lessons from what I read. But the greatest takeaway I got from it today was that the best way to win a war(in some cases) is by not fighting at all. I usually tend to get mad at people who laugh at me or offend me in a certain type of way. Like the other day this guy in my history class that I don't like laughed at my name because the teacher pronounced it "Christina" by accident, He was the only one in the whole class who kept laughing for awhile and maaaaaaannn... I could have roasted this guy so hard, the only stopping me that day was my friend who told me to say nothing. This relates to winning by not fighting at all because by staying quiet I'm ending more conflict, whether it be with my teacher, principal, him, etc. By staying quiet I avoid all those problems and deep inside I know that I have a better future then him so by staying quiet, I win.
