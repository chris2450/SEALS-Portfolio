Blog 2

Posted on March 14, 2018

Today I protested with my fellow students from LACES against the NRA and to regulate gun laws. It was originally supposed to only be a 17 minute event of protesting but a group continued and despite the cops telling us to go back, we kept marching. One thing that I learned from this walkout is that if you want to lead a protest, you have to be really knowlegable about the subject your protesting against. You also have to have a plan of how you're going to execute everything to make sure that you get your mission accomplished. I wasn't able to take the Lead in that protesting group because I didn't have enough of the background knowledge on that particular subject of guns and the NRA. Next time I'm in a protest I'll make sure to fill myself with knowledge on the subject at hand.
